    Illustrated London

    Born from the dreams of a student union at a London university, Inspired by the legendary gatherings of Showmasters, Illustrated London is a vibrant testament to the power of imagination and community. Conceived by friends united in their passion for comics, this open-air fest has evolved into an iconic celebration, blending the art of storytelling with the dynamic energy of 90s rock. From 14th to 20th September 2024, Canary Wharf transforms into a canvas of adventures, where fans and creators converge in a festival of creativity. Echoing decades of fan gatherings, Illustrated London invites you to step into a world where comic legends come to life, and every moment promises a new journey. Join us in this unique legacy, where the spirit of comics unites dreamers of all ages.
